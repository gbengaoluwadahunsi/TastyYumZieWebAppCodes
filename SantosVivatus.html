Santos Vivatus

Santos Vivatus is an accomplished Executive Chef with a passion for creating exceptional dining experiences. With years of experience working in various restaurants, Santos has honed his craft and developed a unique culinary style that is both creative and delicious. At Tasty Yumzie Food Restaurant, Santos oversees the kitchen operations, designing and preparing the menu and ensuring that every dish is expertly crafted.

Santos is dedicated to using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in his dishes. He is committed to supporting local farmers and suppliers and works closely with them to source the best possible ingredients for the restaurant. Santos's expertise in flavor combinations, cooking techniques, and plating presentation ensures that every dish that leaves the kitchen is a work of art.

As Executive Chef, Santos is also responsible for managing the kitchen staff, ensuring that they are well-trained, motivated, and committed to delivering exceptional food and service. He is a natural leader and works closely with the front-of-house team to ensure that service is seamless and guests are satisfied.

Santos's creativity and innovation are evident in every dish he prepares. He is constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques, and he is always looking for ways to push the boundaries of traditional cuisine. Santos's passion for food and his dedication to quality make him a valuable asset to the Tasty Yumzie Food Restaurant team, and his dishes are a highlight of the dining experience.
